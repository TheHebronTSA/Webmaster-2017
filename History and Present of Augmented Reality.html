History of Augmented Reality

  • 1968 - First head mounted display system by Ivan Sutherland and it only showed simple wireframe drawings
  • 1974 - Myron Krueger builds the Video place, an artificial reality laboratory. A room that had projectors and cameras to simulate interactive movement
  • 1990 - Augmented Reality is coined by Tom Caudell, a Boeing Researcher
  • 1992 - Louis Rosenberg creates Virtual Fixtures Earliest Augmented reality systems for the Air Force - Allowed military to control virtually guided machinery in space
  • 1994 - First AR Theatre production by Julie Martin. It was called “Dancing in Cyberspace” and it featured dancers who danced around virtual objects
  • 1998 - First yellow first down marker on a live NFL game
  • 1999 - Naval research on BARS (Battlefield Augmented Reality)
    NASA Flies the Hybrid Syntetic Vision system that uses AR
    Provided map data for flight tests
  • 2000 - ARToolKit - Open source software for overlaying CG on video
  • 2003 - NFL again uses CG to provide the field’s aerial perspective
  • 2009 - More development on ARToolKit for web browsers
    Print media uses AR for the first time
  • 2013 - Used in the Car Systems Volkswagen and Tesla auto drive
  • 2014 - Huge investments in AR and Google creates Google Glasses
  • 2016 - Pokemon Go and the Microsoft HoloLens Dev Kit
